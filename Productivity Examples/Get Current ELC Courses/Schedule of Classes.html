School of Social Sciences

School of Social Sciences comments:

DROP: The deadline to drop courses in the School of Social Sciences is FRIDAY of Week 2 by 5:00PM. Drops can be made in WebReg.

ADD: The deadline to add courses in the School of Social Sciences is FRIDAY of Week 2 by 5:00PM. Adds can be made in WebReg.

CHANGE: The deadline to change grade option or variable units via WebReg for majors in the School of Social Sciences is FRIDAY Week 10 by 5:00PM. Change of grade options or variable units beginning weeks 3-10 must be made through Student Access. Courses for the School's math requirement and major requirements must be completed with letter grade only.

PREREQ: If you received AP/IB/A-level credit or transferred courses that are considered a pre-req, please go to <http://www.undergrad.socsci.uci.edu/ssusa/forms.php> to be cleared in the desired course.

EXCESS UNITS: All students are restricted to 18-unit initially, and will automatically lift to 20 units. If you would like to request more than 20 units and have a UC GPA of at least 3.0, please fill out the form found here: <http://undergrad.socsci.uci.edu/ssusa/forms.php> The School of Social Sciences will wait to process requests until the 18-unit cap is lifted. No exceptions.

RESTRICTIONS: Due to the volume of majors and incoming new students, enrollment in some School of Social Sciences courses will be restricted to School or majors only until THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 1st by noon.

For courses with a "B" or "X" restriction, please contact the instructor of the course.

REPEAT STUDENTS: If you wish to repeat an undergraduate course, please note the repeat restriction will be lifted on THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 1st by noon.
Economics

Economics department comments:

Departmental drop/add policy

Drops

Students may drop Economics courses only through the second week of the quarter. After the second week, permission to drop will NOT be granted by the instructor.

Adds

The Department of Economics' policy on adds remains unchanged, and follows that of the School of Social Sciences.

For more information, please visit the website: http://www.economics.uci.edu/index.php?p=37
